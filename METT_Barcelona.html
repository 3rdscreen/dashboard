“

Barcelona earns most of its traffic organically — Organic Search and Direct together drive 9,286 sessions, a sign of strong brand pull. Paid channels contribute 6,185 channel-attributed sessions (33.8%), led by Cross-network (PMax / Demand Gen). Paid clicks (161,947) far exceed GA4 paid sessions because cross-domain hops to the booking engine and consent drop-off lose attribution — the same effect that prevents property-level revenue tracking.
